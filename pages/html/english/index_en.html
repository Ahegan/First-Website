Discord

An original social network




The objective of this website is quite simple, to present a social network wich is still not very well-known nowadays and as this, is confusing people.

The TF1's case

Just above, a certain confusion in the large public area was mentioned, here is a concrete example :

quand TF1 affirme dans son 20h que Discord est un réseau social crée par Les insoumis. Du lourd pic.twitter.com/cldSbJkuuA

— Cara (@Skuyrtle) September 23, 2017

"The activists of "La France Insoumise" have created their own social network, their own Facebook in a way. As you can see, it looks a little bit like Facebook..."

You can find more informations about the creation of Discord on the section presentation. However, it is necessary to specify that this social network was in no way created by Les Insoumis.

("Les insoumis" are people from "La France Insoumise", an extreme french left political party)

Here is a typical case of misunderstanding, if not misinformation certainly due to a journalist not having studied enought.
