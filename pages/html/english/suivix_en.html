Suivix

Put online in 2020, Suivix is a Bot Discord allowing teachers to take attendance among their students. If you don't know what the term Bot, means, I refer you to the functionalities section.

Developed during the first lockdown by a French student, this Bot is primarily intended for foreign (mostly American) teachers using Discord to work remotely with their students much more than their French counterparts

With a total of 1,860 users, the Bot has found its audience and is installed on 625 servers.

If you want to learn more about this Bot or even if you want to use it, I refer you to the official website
