Presentation

This application, initially created for gamers but which later attracted many communities (developers and musicians for example), was available to users for the first time in 2015. The company was then called Hammer & Chisel Inc.

Discord, as it is now called, was born from a desire to bring together all the VoIP software of the time on a single platform. Whether it was Skype, Mumble or TeamSpeak (to name only the most famous) had indeed all their pros but also their cons :

VoIP or Voice over IP refers to a system capable of transforming from analogue audio signals to digital data, phone calls by internet
    Skype :
  • + simple interface
  • - consumes too many resources
    TeamSpeak :
  • + well optimised, many features
  • - complex interface

During its development, this application gathered what could best meet the needs of the various users (more details in the functionalities section).

Controversy

Due to the great freedom that has always been given to users, Discord is often accused of not moderating its application enough on sensitive subjects such as religion, politics, racism or pornography. In order to calm the controversy, without depriving users of their freedoms, the company will make available at the end of 2016 an NSFW (Not Safe For Work) filter pointing out content that may be offensive or inappropriate.

In 2017, CEO Jason Citron also stated that the company was aware of the presence of malicious servers (more informations in functionalities) and as a result, any servers linked to illegal activities or that do not comply with the conditions of use would be shutdowned and deleted once detected.
